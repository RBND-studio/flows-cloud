# Migrate to Flows

We understand that transitioning from your current solution to Flows can be a daunting task. We're here to help you every step of the way.

## Assisted migration

For a seamless transition, we offer services to help you migrate your existing onboarding solution to Flows. Our team will work with you to understand your current setup and guide you through the migration process.

This service is available to all customers who are looking to switch to Flows. To get started, [contact us](/contact-us) and we'll help you out.

## Cost

The migration service is included in your Flows subscription at no additional cost. We want to make sure that you have a smooth transition to Flows without any extra charges. When transitioning to Flows from another tool, we offer free usage to cover the transition period and avoid double billing.
